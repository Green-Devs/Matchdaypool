Welcome to MatchdayPool!

I.a Purpose of application

Many people are fans of different sports like soccer, football, volleyball, etc. And many of those people like betting, so by nature they end up making some kind of pool related to the sport they enjoy watching. The purpose of the application is to facilitate the making, administering, and sharing of this pools. Users will have the option to join or create pools, it be public to participate with people all around the globe or private to participate with friends only.

I.b Scope

The scope of this project includes from young adults to adults as we know pools are very popular among all age groups. The plan is for the project to be a platform where people can create their own pools for any sport of preference and let them invite other people to participate.

I.c Vision

The aim of this project is to be a useful tool that helps adults to create their own pools for competitive sports or any other things that they may have in mind. Our application proposes to provide people with an easy way to create pools about anything they may want, administer them, and share them with whoever they want.

I.d Objective

  • Facilitate the creation of pools and the sharing of them.
  • Permit users to choose whom to share their pools with.
  • Show all the information about a pool, like theme, teams, members, results, etc.
